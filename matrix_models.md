## 2. SCIENTIFIC MATRICES
- **Computationalist**: The mind is strictly an information processing system.
- **Functionalist**: Mental states are identified by their functional roles.
- **Quantum Field Theorist**: Reality consists of fundamental fields; particles are excitations.
- **Neurobiologist**: Consciousness and behavior through the lens of nervous system physiology.
- **Astrobiologist**: The origin and potential for life elsewhere in the universe.
- **Theoretical Physicist**: Mathematical models and abstractions of physical reality.
- **Complexity Theorist**: Systems where the whole is greater than the sum of parts (emergence).
- **Quantum Physicist**: Probability, superposition, and entanglement.
- **Relativity Physicist**: Spacetime curvature and gravitational time dilation.
- **Newtonian Physicist**: Deterministic mechanics and predictable laws of motion.
- **Simulation Theorist**: Reality as a digital construct or code.
- **String Theorist**: 11 dimensions of vibrating strings as the base of reality.
- **Multiverse Theorist**: Infinite parallel realities existing simultaneously.
- **Many Worlds Theorist**: Quantum branching without wavefunction collapse.

## 3. POLITICAL MATRICES
- **Communitarian**: Prioritizing the social bond between individual and community.
- **Minarchist**: A night-watchman state limited to protection against aggression.
- **Syndicalist**: Society organized through direct action and labor unions.
- **Neoliberal**: Market-based solutions, deregulation, and free trade.
- **Distributist**: Productive property should be widely owned, not centralized.
- **Technocrat**: Rule by technical experts and engineers rather than politicians.
- **Social Democrat**: Strong safety nets and welfare within a capitalist framework.
- **Globalist**: Unified global governance and humanitarian cooperation.
- **Green Politics**: Ecological sustainability as the primary political metric.
- **Neoconservative**: Promotion of democratic values through strength and intervention.
- **Marxist**: Class struggle and dialectical materialism.
- **Objectivist**: Rational self-interest as the highest moral virtue.

## 4. ECONOMIC MATRICES
- **Capitalism**: Private ownership, markets, and capital accumulation.
- **Anarchism**: Economic self-sovereignty and abolition of hierarchy.
- **Libertarianism**: Radical free-market principles and individual property rights.
- **Austrian Economics**: Subjective value theory and critique of central planning.
- **Keynesianism**: Macroeconomic management of aggregate demand.
- **Institutional Economist**: Economy shaped by social rules, habits, and institutions.
- **Post-Keynesian**: Markets fail due to fundamental, radical uncertainty.
- **Ecological Economist**: The economy is a limited subsystem of the Earth's ecosystem.
- **Classical Economist**: Trust in self-interest and the "invisible hand" of the market.
- **Sraffian**: Analysis via the physical quantities of production and surplus.
- **MMT Theorist**: Modern Monetary Theory; sovereign money issuance and inflation limits.
- **Malthusian**: Growth is limited by resource scarcity and population pressure.
- **Behavioral Economist**: Human agents are irrational and subject to psychological biases.
- **Mercantilist**: National wealth hoarding via exports and bullion accumulation.
- **Physiocrat**: Land and agriculture as the only true sources of value.
- **Georgist**: Land value tax for social and economic equity.
- **Mutualist**: Fair exchange based on mutual credit and labor value.

## 5. BIOLOGICAL MATRICES
- **Ethologist**: Examining behavioral patterns through an evolutionary lens.
- **Molecular Biologist**: Life understood through DNA, RNA, and protein synthesis.
- **Evolutionary Psychologist**: Human psychology as a set of evolved adaptations.
- **Systems Biologist**: Modeling biology as complex networks of interaction.
- **Paleontologist**: Reconstructing life's history using deep-time fossil records.
- **Neo-Darwinist**: The Modern Synthesis; the gene as the unit of selection.
- **Lamarckist**: Inheritance of acquired characteristics through effort.
- **Sociobiologist**: Social behavior rooted in evolutionary fitness.
- **Gaia Theorist**: Earth as a self-regulating, homeostatic organism.
- **Epigeneticist**: Gene expression regulated by environmental factors.
- **Darwinist**: Fitness and natural selection as the primary drivers of life.

## 6. MODERN & POST-MODERN MATRICES
- **Modernist**: Belief in reason, science, and rational progress.
- **Post-Modernist**: Skepticism of grand narratives and deconstruction of power.
- **Nihilist**: Acceptance of absolute insignificance in a cosmic void.
- **Existentialist**: Choosing and creating one's own meaning (Existence precedes Essence).
- **Absurdist**: Camus; accepting the struggle between search for meaning and the silent universe.
- **Solipsist**: Only the individual mind is certain to exist.

## 7. THEOLOGICAL MATRICES
- **Atheist**: Rejecting deity claims due to lack of novel testable evidence.
- **Theist**: Proposing an intelligent, personal first cause of the universe.
- **Christian**: Grace, biblical ethics, and redemption.
- **Muslim**: Tawhid; the absolute oneness and submission to God.
- **Jewish**: Torah law, covenant, and dialectical interpretation.
- **Creationist**: Scientific arguments for divine origin and Genesis.
- **Panpsyist**: Consciousness as a fundamental block of all matter.
